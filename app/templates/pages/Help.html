How do I find new recipe?

Click on Home button and you will be directed to a page where you can find all new recipes.
How do I add new Recipe?

When you click on Add Recipe tab from the menu you will be directed to a page where user can add their recipe.
How do I update my profile?

Under the profile tab select edit setting where user will be able to update their profile information.
Where can I find my favorite recipe?

Favorite recipe is located under profile tab named My Favorite
How do I like(favorite) and dislike recipes?

From Home page where you see new recipe there is two oprion on right and left to like and dislike recipes.
How do I add new ingredients into pantry?

You can add new Ingredients into the system from the form under the Add Ingredients tab.
Where can I find list of available ingredients?

List of Available ingredients is available under profile tab on the My Pantry
